Pretentious Indie Band was founded in 2010 by Lou Paniccia, a longtime member of the team behind Sonicbids and drummer for several indie rock bands. Lou realized there had to be a middle ground for bands looking to make it that was somewhere between signing away 15% to a manager who would handle everything and simply going it alone, 1 band vs. the world.

Indie artists don't need to be isolationist.

What if several successful independent bands joined forces to create their own management company? They could pool resources, experience, contacts and get the benefits of being part of a larger organization while still keeping their independence as artists.

Today, PiB Management represents a cooperative roster of artists making waves in their respective markets, driven by a central support team as well as their own manpower. Who says indie bands have to go it alone?
